Catharsis Reborn

Catharsis Reborn is a total conversion mod for one of the worst games ever made, Postal III, aiming to turn it into an actually good game. To do this, Catharsis Reborn boasts an all new story and campaign, weapons, and a variety of new content and mechanics! Such as the inventory system and insult system from Postal 2! In addition to a lot of bug and crash fixes!
