New Website!

Nov 5, 2022 - Written by Pseudonym_Tim



I decided that it was time to ditch my old website and create a new one, so here we are! It's a bit rough at the time of writing this, but I think it's pretty good for what I have in mind right now... Real professional looking! Eventually I think I'll add pages for my music and artwork...

Anywho, If it wasn't already obvious, this website is basically just a glorified personal portfolio. A place where I can show off all the things I worked on, and host downloads of stuff...

Oh yeah, this is the first blog post on the website! I thought that it'd be neat to have a blog, a place to basically ramble about anything I want! Going forward, I'm going to try to make semi-frequent blog posts about various things, maybe someone will find some stuff I write about interesting, maybe not. It's probably just going to be a lot of programming stuff, technical post-mortems and things like that. Either way it's an opportunity to satisfy the mansplaining demons in my head!

Alright, well I still have some stuff to do before I consider this website finished, mostly organization and cleanup stuff... bye-bye!
